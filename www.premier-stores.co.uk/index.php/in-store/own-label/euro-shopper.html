Euro Shopper

Now, the fastest growing convenience brand in the UK, Euro Shopper is a range of over 90 great value everyday family favourites.

There is a great choice available, from soft drinks to ice cream, chocolate to biscuits you can be sure they taste good and offer great value for money.

There is also a fantastic range of chilled, all the essentials such as Euro Shopper milk cheese, ham and bacon are readily available. As well as non-food such as washing up liquid, bleach, toilet rolls and kitchen rolls essentials to name but a few.

Look out for the red and white packs with a yellow price mark – you can spot the great value – with lots of products priced just 50p and under.

*Not all products available at all Premier stores
